# Ethics, data use, and licensing notes

## Source material

The collection is derived from publicly accessible research papers and lecture
videos. Copyright remains with the original authors, publishers, and video
creators. Paper figures and transcript text are used for research evaluation
and source identification; their presence in an index does not transfer or
replace the original licenses. Anyone redistributing the artifact should verify
the applicable terms for each source and prefer reconstruction scripts over
redistributing source media.

## Automated processing

- Indonesian lecture transcripts are automatically generated captions and may
  contain recognition errors or language-specific quality differences.
- Some missing figure captions are generated with LLaVA-7B and may be inaccurate.
- Relevance judgments are produced by a single LLM assessor and are explicitly
  provisional. They may reflect model bias and are not a substitute for bilingual
  human assessment.
- The bounded review evaluation measures structural grounding only. It does not
  establish factual correctness, absence of hallucination, or review quality.

## Expected use and risks

The system is a research prototype, not a production scholarly-search service.
Language, register, modality, and transcription quality can change which sources
are retrieved. Generated reviews should therefore be checked against the cited
source material before academic use.

## Project license

No project-wide software or data license is asserted by these notes. The author
must choose and add an appropriate repository license before public release,
while preserving all third-party licenses and attribution requirements.
